Ultrasonic Metal Welding Machines

Ultrasonic for Metal welding machine

Metal welding is used in the automotive and electronics industries to produce batteries, cable connections, etc. In metal welding, metals are welded in high temperatures, for instance. We differentiate between metal active gas (MAG) and metal inert gas (MIG) welding. Active gases from a connection with the metal, inert gases do not. In ultrasonic welding, ultrasonic waves are used to join the metals.
◉ Cylinder battery welding
◉ Pouch cells welding
◉ Electrical car application

Ultrasonic welding components

Ultrasonic welding technology is gaining in importance and is an increasingly frequent integral component in modern manufacturing processes. As an efficient, material-friendly and sustainable joining technique, it is ideally suited for joining thermoplastics or non-ferrous metals. Many mechanical engineering components use this technology advantage when processing plastics or metals in customized machines. Robust and reliable ultrasonic components are a requirement for this. Linked together on a mechanical basis, the converter, booster, and sonotrode create an ultrasonic stack with a determined acoustic resonance. All of the ultrasonic components we provide receive a 100% quality check: ‘’Made in Germany’’.
